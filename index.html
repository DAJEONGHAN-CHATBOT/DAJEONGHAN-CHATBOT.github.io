한줄성경 is an AI chatbot designed to enhance the experience of searching for Bible verses. It provides emotionally resonant scripture-based answers to personal concerns and major life decisions, fostering faith-driven reflection while offering a comforting, interactive experience.

Abstract

"주의 말씀은 내 발의 등이요 내 길의 빛이니이다." ┃ *"Your word is a lamp to my feet and a light for my path."* — 시편 119:105 (Psalm 119:105)

성경은 세대를 넘어 삶의 방향을 제시하며, 혼란 속에서 길을 찾는 이들에게 등불이 되어 왔습니다. ┃ *For generations, the Bible has been a guiding light for those seeking clarity in times of uncertainty.*

AI 기술이 발전하며 대화형 모델이 단순한 정보 검색을 넘어 **사람의 고민을 이해하고 맞춤형 응답을 제공하는 시대**가 되었습니다. ┃ *With advancements in AI, conversational models are now capable of understanding human concerns and delivering contextually relevant responses.*

**성경챗봇**은 사용자의 질문에 맞춰 성경의 깊은 메시지를 제공하는 AI 기반 신앙 동반자입니다. ┃ *BibleBot is an AI-powered faith companion that delivers scripture-based insights tailored to individual inquiries.*

인간관계, 신앙적 고민, 삶의 중요한 선택 등 다양한 순간에서 **성경챗봇은 단순한 검색 도구를 넘어 살아 있는 대화 상대가 됩니다.** ┃ *From relationships to spiritual struggles and life decisions, BibleBot serves not just as a search tool, but as an interactive guide to biblical wisdom.*

AI와 신학이 만나는 이 새로운 방식은 **디지털 시대에도 신앙이 더욱 가까이 다가갈 수 있도록 돕습니다.** ┃ *By merging AI with theology, we bridge timeless scripture with modern seekers, ensuring that faith remains accessible in the digital age.*

Related Links

There's a lot of excellent work that was introduced around the same time as ours.

Progressive Encoding for Neural Optimization introduces an idea similar to our windowed position encoding for coarse-to-fine optimization.

D-NeRF and NR-NeRF both use deformation fields to model non-rigid scenes.

Some works model videos with a NeRF by directly modulating the density, such as Video-NeRF, NSFF, and DyNeRF

There are probably many more by the time you are reading this. Check out Frank Dellart's survey on recent NeRF papers, and Yen-Chen Lin's curated list of NeRF papers.
